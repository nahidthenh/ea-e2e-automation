## Notes

- Widgets with 3rd-party dependencies (Contact Form 7, Gravity Forms, Ninja Forms, WPForms, Fluent Form, Caldera Forms, WeForms, Formstack, TypeForm, Mailchimp, LearnDash, WooCommerce, Facebook Feed, Instagram Feed, Twitter Feed, EmbedPress, BetterDocs, NFT Gallery) require those plugins/services active in the test environment.
- To generate a test for any widget run: `/ea-widget-tests <widget-slug>`
- Example: `/ea-widget-tests advanced-tabs`
